How it Works?

  1. Download and Join

    Download the reef app, sign up and take full advantage of
    our network of venues across the globe.

  2. Login & create

    Create your profile and book your workspace.
    Customise your profile to showcase your professional skills
    and collaborate with like-minded remote workers.

  3. Search & book

    Search for venues near you. Sort by location, amenities and ratings.

  4. Work & enjoy

    Once you’ve found your venue of choice, book your spot. Favourite the venues you like, review previous and amend upcoming bookings with ease.
